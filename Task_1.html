Hi i am from Jharkhand, the places that you must visit here are:

Giridih

I am Chandan Raj from Giridih District. Giridih has an average elevation of 289 metres (948 feet).
Sri Sammeta Shikharji also known as the Parasnath Hills,it is one of the holiest place of Jainism.
It is a conical granite peak located 4,477 feet (1,382 metres) above the sea level.

Tata Nagar

The city was founded by the late Jamshedji Nusserwanji Tata.
It is also called Steel City, TataNagar or simply Tata.
Here you can visit the industry of TATA, NIT Jamshedpur(one of the top most engineering college in INDIA).

Dhanbad

Dhandad, the city of black daimond is one of the most beautiful place.
Famous for its coal reserves, and thus known as the Coal Capital of India,
Dhanbad is popular for its pretty travel destinations.
Here you can also visit the school of mining i.e IIT Dhandad

Ranchi

One of the oldest cities of Jharkhand,
Ranchi used to be the summer capital of Bihar during British rule.
Popularly known as the City of Waterfalls,
Ranchi is famous for the many picturesque falls,
making it a popular tourist attraction.
It also has very old and deep historical and cultural roots.
